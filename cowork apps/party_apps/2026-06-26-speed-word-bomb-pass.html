Bomb Pass

💣 The pass-the-phone word fuse game
Say a word containing the letters on screen, then smack PASS to the next player — before the bomb blows up in someone's hands. 3–10 players · ~5–10 min a game.

How to Play

1️⃣ Sit in a circle and pass one phone/laptop around.
2️⃣ The screen shows a letter chunk (like TR or ING).
3️⃣ On your turn, say any real word that contains those letters in order.
4️⃣ Smack the big green PASS button and hand the device to the next player.
5️⃣ The bomb has a hidden fuse — whoever's holding it when it BOOMS loses a life.
6️⃣ Run out of lives and you're out. Last player standing wins! 👑
No repeats in a round (honor system). Stuck? You can still pass — but a bad/empty pass is risky karma. Keep it fast!
