🎨 3–10 players · 20–30 min · pass-the-device · one screen for the room

How to play

One player holds the device and taps to see a secret word plus a random Dare — a silly drawing handicap. They draw on the screen while the timer runs and everyone else shouts guesses out loud. As soon as someone nails it, the drawer taps Guessed! and picks who got it. The guesser and the drawer both score. Pass the device to the next player and keep going. First to the target score wins the Golden Pencil. 🏆
