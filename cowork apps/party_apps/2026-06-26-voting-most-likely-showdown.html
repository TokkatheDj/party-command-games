SHOWDOWN

The whole room points at one person. The screen keeps score. Chaos & laughs ensue.

👥 3–10 players ⏱ ~15–25 min 📺 One screen, everyone plays 👨‍👩‍👧 Family-friendly

No internet, no apps, no accounts — just this screen and your crew.

‹ Back

How to Play

1. Add everyone's name on the next screen.

2. A prompt appears, like “Most likely to become a famous TikToker.”

3. On the count of 3… 2… 1… POINT! everyone points at the person they think fits best.

4. Whoever got the most fingers pointed at them — tap their name on the screen. They score a point (tap again for ties or extra votes).

5. First to the target score wins the Showdown. 👑

Host tip: One person holds the screen and taps the round's winner. It's fast — keep it moving!
