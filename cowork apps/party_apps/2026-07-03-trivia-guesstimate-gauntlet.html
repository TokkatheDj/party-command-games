Guess-timate Gauntlet

Nobody knows the answer — the closest guess wins!
3–10 players  •  ~15–25 minutes  •  one shared screen, host taps

How to play (read once, aloud)

  1. A number question appears — everyone shouts or writes a guess. No phones!
  2. Host taps Reveal, then taps whoever guessed closest (+2). Tap again if they nailed it exactly (+1 bonus). Ties? Tap them all.
  3. Most points after the final round takes the crown.
