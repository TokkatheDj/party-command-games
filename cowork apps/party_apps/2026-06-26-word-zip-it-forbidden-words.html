Word Game · 4–10 players · ~15–25 min

ZIP IT!

Get your team to guess the secret word — but you can't say any of the five forbidden words. Beat the clock, dodge the foul buzzer, climb the scoreboard.

No internet needed · works offline · family-friendly

How to Play

  • One clue-giver per team holds the device and sees the secret word + 5 forbidden words.
  • Describe the secret word so teammates shout it out — without saying any forbidden word (or the word itself).
  • Tap GOT IT for each correct guess (+1). Tap SKIP to pass (no penalty). Tap FOUL if a forbidden word slips out (−1).
  • Keep going until the timer runs out, then pass the device to the next team.
  • First team to the target score wins!
