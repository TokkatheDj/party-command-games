🔥 HOT SEAT HUNCH

One player takes the Hot Seat. Everyone else points LEFT or RIGHT to guess their real answer!

👥 3–10 players  •  ⏱ ~15–25 minutes  •  🃏 90 questions

How a round works:
1. The screen names the Hot Seat player and shows a this-or-that question.
2. Hot Seat stays quiet! On the 3-2-1 countdown, everyone else points LEFT or RIGHT to guess their answer.
3. Hot Seat taps their true answer — correct guessers get +1.
4. Fooled most of the room? Hot Seat earns a +2 "Hard to Read" bonus!
