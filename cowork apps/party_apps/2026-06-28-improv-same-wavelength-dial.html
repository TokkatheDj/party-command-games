One player sees a secret spot on the dial and gives a single clue. Their team turns the needle to find it. Closer = more points.

🎉 3–10 players · 2–4 teams · ~15–25 min

How it works
  1. A team's Reader taps to peek at the hidden target zone (everyone else looks away).
  2. They see a spectrum like Cold ↔ Hot and give one word or phrase that points to the secret spot.
  3. The team debates, then drags the needle and locks it in.
  4. Land in the bullseye for 4, near it for 3 or 2. First team to 20 wins!
