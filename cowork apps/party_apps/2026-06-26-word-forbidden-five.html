FORBIDDEN FIVE

Describe the secret word to your team — but you can't say any of the five forbidden words. Race the clock, rack up points, dodge the buzzer.

4–10 players · 2 teams · ~15–30 min

How to Play

1. Split into two teams. Each round, one player is the clue-giver and holds this device so only they can see the screen.

2. The big word on top is what your team must guess. Describe it out loud — but never say the word itself or any of the 5 forbidden words below it.

3. Team guesses it? Tap Got it ✓ (+1). Stuck? Tap Skip (no points, no penalty).

4. A watcher from the other team looks over your shoulder. Slip up and say a forbidden word? They tap Buzz (–1 and next card).

5. When the timer ends, pass the device to the other team. First team to the target score wins!
