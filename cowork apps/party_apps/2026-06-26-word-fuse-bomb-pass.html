FUSE

Hot Potato Word Bomb. A category appears, the fuse is lit — shout an answer, smack PASS, and get rid of the bomb before it blows!
👥 3–10 players  •  ⏱ 10–20 min  •  pass-the-device
Family-friendly • No internet needed • Sound on for the full effect 🔊

How to play

1. Sit in a circle and pick how many are playing.

2. A category shows on screen with a lit bomb. The fuse length is a secret!

3. The holder must say one answer out loud that fits the category, then tap the giant PASS button to hand the device to the next person.

4. No repeats, no stalling — others can challenge a bad answer.

5. 💥 Whoever is holding the device when the bomb explodes earns a strike.

6. Three strikes and you're out. Last player standing wins!
