Lightning Categories

Host-run team trivia. Reveal a question, tap the team that nailed it, race up the scoreboard — then bet it all in the Final round.

👥 3–10 players (2–4 teams)  •  ⏱️ 15–25 min  •  📺 One screen, one host

How it works: Split into 2–4 teams. The host reads each question aloud. First team to shout the right answer earns the points (easy → hard, worth more each step). The host taps that team to award them. End on the Final Wager — teams bet their points on one tough question.

No internet needed • Family-friendly • 60+ questions across 12 categories
